How to Get Support & DSA Funding

1. Contact Your University Disability Team

Tell them about your condition and needs. They’ll guide you through the process and write a support recommendation.

2. Apply for Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA)

Fill in the online form on the SAAS website. You’ll need medical evidence (e.g. doctor’s letter, diagnostic report).

3. Attend a Needs Assessment (free)

An assessor recommends equipment and support. This is fully funded — you don’t pay anything.

4. Receive Your Funding & Support

SAAS approves your DSA. Suppliers deliver equipment, mentors are arranged, and adjustments are put in place.

Common Reasonable Adjustments

Extended exam time (25–100%)

Quiet or separate exam room

Note-taker or lecture recordings

BSL interpreter or electronic notetaker

Accessible formats (Braille, large print, audio)

Mentoring & study skills support
