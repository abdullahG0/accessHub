About AccessHub Scotland

Simple, clear help for every disabled student in Scotland — made by people who understand.

Who We Are

We are an independent, student-led project created to make university life easier for disabled students across Scotland.

We are not part of any university or government — we just want to help you get the support you deserve, in the simplest way possible.

What We Do

We explain everything in plain English (no confusing jargon) including:

  • How to apply for Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA)
  • What technology and help you can get for free
  • How to get extra time in exams and other adjustments
  • Accessible housing, transport and campus information
  • Your rights under the Equality Act

Why We Do This

Many disabled students miss out on support because the information is hard to find or too complicated.

We believe every disabled student should:

  • Know their rights
  • Get the funding they’re entitled to
  • Feel confident and included at university

Have a question? We’re here to help — no question is too small.
