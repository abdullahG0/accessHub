About AccessHub Scotland

Scotland’s leading independent resource for disability inclusion in higher education

Our Mission

To ensure every disabled student in Scotland can access higher education on equal terms — through clear information, strong rights awareness, and practical support.

We work with students, universities, SAAS, and disability organisations to remove barriers and promote best practice across all 19 Scottish higher education institutions.

Your Rights – Scottish & UK Law

Equality Act 2010

All Scottish universities must make reasonable adjustments and not discriminate against disabled students in admission, teaching, or services.

Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA)

Non-repayable funding from SAAS covering specialist equipment, support workers, software, and travel costs — available to undergraduates and postgraduates.

BSL (Scotland) Act 2015

Promotes British Sign Language and requires public authorities (including universities) to publish BSL plans and provide interpretation when needed.

UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities

The UK (including Scotland) is fully signed up — guaranteeing the right to inclusive education at all levels.
