Your Rights Under Scottish & UK Law

The Equality Act 2010 requires all Scottish universities to make reasonable adjustments and remove barriers for disabled students. You are also entitled to Disabled Students’ Allowance (DSA) from SAAS — non-repayable funding for equipment, support workers, software, and travel.
