FAQ & Contact Us

Quick answers to the most common questions — or just say hello!

Do I have to pay for DSA equipment?

No — everything is 100% free. SAAS pays for laptops, software, support workers, taxis, etc.

Can I apply for DSA before I start university?

Yes! You can apply as soon as you have an offer — even conditionally.

What counts as medical evidence?

A letter from your GP, consultant, or a diagnosis letter is enough. No need for a long report.

Will telling my university affect my grades?

No. Disability information is confidential and cannot affect marking or academic judgement.

I’m autistic/ADHD/dyslexic — am I eligible?

Yes! Neurodiversity, mental health conditions, and chronic illnesses all qualify for DSA and adjustments.

What if my university isn’t helping?

Contact Lead Scotland (free helpline) or the Equality & Human Rights Commission. They can support you.

Still have a question?

We reply within 24 hours — no question is too small.
