Campus Access

Everything you need to move around campus safely and independently

Your university must make campus accessible — it’s the law

Under the Equality Act 2010, every Scottish university has to remove barriers for disabled students.

Common Campus Support

Accessible Housing

Adapted rooms (wheelchair, hearing loops, flashing alarms) at the same price band as standard rooms.

Parking

Blue Badge spaces close to buildings — free with your badge.

Lifts & Ramps

All main buildings must be reachable without stairs.

Accessible Toilets

Radar key access, hoists, and adult changing places on most campuses.

Quiet & Sensory Rooms

Calm spaces for autistic students or anyone who gets overwhelmed.

Emergency Evacuation Help

Personal Emergency Evacuation Plans (PEEPs) — staff know how to help you in a fire alarm.

Travel & Getting Around

Taxi Funding via DSA

If buses/trains aren’t accessible, DSA can pay for taxis to and from campus.

Campus Shuttle Buses

Most universities have wheelchair-accessible minibuses — ask your disability team.

Assistance Dogs

Fully welcome everywhere on campus.
